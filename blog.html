Germany Opportunity Card

The Germany Opportunity Card (Chancenkarte) allows qualified professionals to move to Germany without a job offer and search for employment for up to 12 months.

Benefits

  • No job offer required
  • Stay in Germany while searching
  • Work up to 20 hours/week
  • Attend interviews
  • Professional networking
  • Convert to Work Visa or EU Blue Card

Eligibility

  • Recognized qualification
  • Relevant work experience
  • English or German language skills
  • Proof of financial resources

Ausbildung – Earn While You Learn

Ausbildung combines classroom learning with paid practical training.

Benefits

  • Monthly salary
  • Practical work experience
  • No university degree for many courses
  • Excellent job prospects
  • Long-term employment opportunities

Popular Programs

  • Nursing
  • IT
  • Retail
  • Hospitality
  • Logistics
  • Electrical
  • Mechatronics
  • Industrial Mechanics
  • Automotive

Family Reunion Visa

Family members living legally in Germany may sponsor eligible relatives through the Family Reunion Visa.

Requirements

  • Proof of relationship
  • Financial stability
  • Suitable accommodation
  • Basic German language skills (for spouses)
